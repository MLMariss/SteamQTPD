**Playtime backfill.** Raw playtime covers **61,888 games (50.2% of catalog)**. The other **44,558 games are below the floor and correctly skipped** (too few reviews to produce a sentiment-split median).

**Summarizers now run on every raw pass.** Raw 61,888 · summarized 61,726 · rated 61,720 (current gap 162). `playtime_summarize.py` and `ratings_summarize.py` are chained into `playtime-raw.yml`, so the small frontend files refresh right after the shards do (8×/day). Any residual gap is just the within-run ordering between the shard commit and the summarize steps, not a stalled pipeline.

**HLTB estimates refresh live.** **18,725 of 123,222 `hltb.json` entries carry the `est` flag** (15.2%), leaving **104,497 reals (84.8%)**. `hltb_estimate.py` is a shared helper imported by `hltb_refresh.py` (as `HE`) and called in the fill loop, so estimates recompute from live median ratios every 2h as new reals land — it is not a stale one-off. (The old one-off `hltb_backfill.py` sweep was removed; the live path took over.)

**Rating % / review count.** Rating % is present for **112,168 games (91.0%)**; review count for **115,470 (93.7%)**. The gap is titles with too few reviews to carry a meaningful score.

**Tags** are non-empty for **83,448 games (67.7%)**. The remainder are untagged on SteamSpy (typically very-low-review or unreleased titles).
