**Playtime backfill.** Raw playtime covers **39,301 games (31.9% of catalog)**. The honest denominator is the addressable set after the `MIN_REVIEWS_FLOOR = 10` gate — **78,639 games (63.9% of catalog)** — against which raw is **50.0% of addressable**. The other **44,378 games are below the floor and correctly skipped** (too few reviews to produce a sentiment-split median).

**Summarizers now run on every raw pass.** Raw 39,301 · summarized 39,294 · rated 39,290 (current gap 7). `playtime_summarize.py` and `ratings_summarize.py` are chained into `playtime-raw.yml`, so the small frontend files refresh right after the shards do (8×/day). Any residual gap is just the within-run ordering between the shard commit and the summarize steps, not a stalled pipeline.

**HLTB estimates refresh live.** **17,899 of 122,991 `hltb.json` entries carry the `est` flag** (14.6%), leaving **105,092 reals (85.4%)**. `hltb_estimate.py` is a shared helper imported by `hltb_refresh.py` (as `HE`) and called in the fill loop, so estimates recompute from live median ratios every 2h as new reals land — it is not a stale one-off. (The old one-off `hltb_backfill.py` sweep was removed; the live path took over.)

**Rating % / review count.** Rating % is present for **112,107 games (91.1%)**; review count for **115,405 (93.8%)**. The gap is titles with too few reviews to carry a meaningful score.

**Tags** are non-empty for **83,445 games (67.8%)**. The remainder are untagged on SteamSpy (typically very-low-review or unreleased titles).
