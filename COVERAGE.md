Any residual gap is just the within-run ordering between the shard commit and the summarize steps, not a stalled pipeline.

(The old one-off `hltb_backfill.py` sweep was removed; the live path took over.)

**Rating % / review count.** Rating % is present for **112,154 games (91.0%)**; review count for **115,452 (93.7%)**. The gap is titles with too few reviews to carry a meaningful score.
