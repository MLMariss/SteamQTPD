Any residual gap is just the within-run ordering between the shard commit and the summarize steps, not a stalled pipeline.

**HLTB estimates refresh live.** **18,726 of 123,232 `hltb.json` entries carry the `est` flag** (15.2%), leaving **104,506 reals (84.8%)**. `hltb_estimate.py` is a shared helper imported by `hltb_refresh.py` (as `HE`) and called in the fill loop, so estimates recompute from live median ratios every 2h as new reals land — it is not a stale one-off. (The old one-off `hltb_backfill.py` sweep was removed; the live path took over.)

**Rating % / review count.** Rating % is present for **112,178 games (91.0%)**; review count for **115,480 (93.7%)**. The gap is titles with too few reviews to carry a meaningful score.
