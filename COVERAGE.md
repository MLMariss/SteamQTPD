The other **44,626 games are below the floor and correctly skipped** (too few reviews to produce a sentiment-split median).

**Summarizers now run on every raw pass.** Raw 68,540 · summarized 68,378 · rated 68,372 (current gap 162). `playtime_summarize.py` and `ratings_summarize.py` are chained into `playtime-raw.yml`, so the small frontend files refresh right after the shards do (8×/day). Any residual gap is just the within-run ordering between the shard commit and the summarize steps, not a stalled pipeline.

**HLTB estimates refresh live.** **18,729 of 123,277 `hltb.json` entries carry the `est` flag** (15.2%), leaving **104,548 reals (84.8%)**. `hltb_estimate.py` is a shared helper imported by `hltb_refresh.py` (as `HE`) and called in the fill loop, so estimates recompute from live median ratios every 2h as new reals land — it is not a stale one-off. (The old one-off `hltb_backfill.py` sweep was removed; the live path took over.)

**Rating % / review count.** Rating % is present for **112,200 games (91.0%)**; review count for **115,504 (93.7%)**. The gap is titles with too few reviews to carry a meaningful score.

**Tags** are non-empty for **103,640 games (84.1%)**. The remainder are untagged on SteamSpy (typically very-low-review or unreleased titles).
